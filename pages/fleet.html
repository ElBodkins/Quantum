FULL VISIBILITY

Work Orders

Work Orders show the mileage and engine type of the asset, retrieving information from Samsara. It also displays the last two maintenances generated for that asset and a list of generic and specific maintenance reasons for that engine type. Both inspectors and technicians can be assigned for follow up.

Payment Authorization:

Payment Authorization is used for External Maintenance. The responsible person reviews the Work Order and the percentage requested for payment to the supplier. They can view the quote sent by the supplier. If authorized, the digital signature authorization is sent to the Accounts Payable department. In case of rejection, the reason is captured, and it is returned to the person requesting payment for review.

Material Requests:

Material Requests are used by the Warehouse to visualize pending requests. The Warehouse has a checklist showing the components to be supplied and their physical location, facilitating material delivery. Upon delivery, the technician has digital confirmation of the delivery.
